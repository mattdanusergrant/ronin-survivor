⛩️ MAP BUILDER

How it works
The Dojo is pinned at the center. Every other building is a zone boss chained to a parent — its road is barricaded until the parent falls. Branching is allowed: give one parent several children.

Place — pick a type on the left, then click the map. New buildings chain to the selected building.
Select / move — click a building; drag to move it.
Re-chain — select a building, press Set parent, click the new parent.
Pan / zoom — drag empty space / mouse wheel.
Check — runs the real world generator and verifies every gate seal.
Playtest — saves the map and opens the game with it.

Keep buildings ~1100+ apart (corridors need solid forest between zones) and inside the dashed circle.
