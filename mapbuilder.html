⛩️ MAP BUILDER

Grid map painter. Paint terrain and drop entities cell-by-cell — what you paint is exactly what you play.

Paint — pick a tool on the left, then click/drag on the grid.
Terrain — Floor (walkable), Forest & Water (block movement; shots fly over).
Entities — drop the Dojo, zone buildings, summoner Nests, Camps/Patrols, Campfires, the player Spawn, and Gates.
Gate — a barricade that opens when its owner boss falls. On export each gate is auto-linked to the nearest building; paint a line of gates across the approach to a zone.
Erase — right-click (removes an entity, else clears to forest).
Pan — drag with the middle mouse or hold Space. Zoom — wheel.

Needs exactly one Dojo and one Spawn. Buildings get a depth in the order you place them (deeper = tougher boss); nests/camps inherit the nearest building's depth.
